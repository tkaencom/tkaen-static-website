Tkaen is UAE’s first hybrid Social media/E-commerce experience. An App built to provide a platform & create a community for Individual Sellers & Businesses looking to offer their products & services to Buyers. Create connections between buyers & Sellers for a more personalized experience.

Who can use Tkaen?

Anyone with an interest to sell their talents and e-commerce users who love easy and quick shopping

BUSINESS
OWNERS

There is something for everyone here. From local business owners to established enterprises, all sorts of establishments are welcome to join. While you get to promote your store we assist people find and purchase your products.

BUYERS

Buyers are the core foundation of this app.They may choose from a wide range of products and services that are made available to meet their needs.

SERVICE
PROVIDERS

Have a talent/service you wish to provide but don't know how to widen your scope? No problem. We got you covered. Any type of service are welcome to promote their service and connect with potential customers.

What makes Tkaen Unique?

Personalized Feed

A personalized homepage for users wherein you can comment, forward, like, save, interact with other users. And not to forget a trending products tab to showcase the top favourites of many.

Chat with businesses & users.

Not only can you communicate with other users via the messaging icon, but you can also communicate with the business owners. A high functioning messaging icon to have a more personal and immediate connection with your seller.

Profiles for everyone

As a user you may choose to either sell or purchase. But regardless of the type of user you are you have the freedom to customize and create your personal profile page.

Easy Search and Information

This app is made to make your experience of booking a service or buying a product hassle free and easy. All the Information you need is made available at your fingertips.

Explore

From the most recent deals to a variety of product and service categories, find everything you need at your convenience.
